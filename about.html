Our Founder

Our founder is Gavin Schroeder. He was a student enrolled at Wctc and had a fondness for recoreds and record collecting. He soon realised records were becoming cool again so he opened a shop near his home town to turn his hobby into a business

Location

Waukesha is a very popluated area with not a lot of areas to actually record shop, this combined with the nice atmosphere of downtown wukaesha made it the perfect place to set up

Values

it is our goal to make record shopping easy and affordable, it is also our goal to maximise preservation of all vinyl records.
